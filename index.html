Collect, share and discuss local knowledge.

GeoKey is an infrastructure for participatory mapping.

Communities Collect exactly the data your community needs.

Whether you want to map the history of your neighbourhood, collect data to identify issues that should be fixed by your local government or simply map hidden gems in your area—GeoKey can help.

  • Setup up your data structures
  • Decide who can access, contribute and moderate data
  • Add photos and videos to your contributions
  • Discuss

Start mapping today

Developers Build and connect apps to collect, visualise and analyse data.

Use our public API to create your own mobile app to collect data, visualise your data in a GIS or run complex statistical analysis on your data.

  • Use the REST API to build apps
  • Install GeoKey on your server

Start coding

Fancy a try?

We have set up a testing service for GeoKey that you can use to give it a try. You can sign up, create projects and connect to the API. Just don't use it in production, we'll clean the data from time to time.

About GeoKey

GeoKey has been developed at Extreme Citizen Science Research Group at University College London. The development of the initial version was funded by FP-7 project Citizen Cyberlab.
