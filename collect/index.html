Collect data

Use these applications to connect to GeoKey and collect data with your community.

Community Maps

Mapping for Change has been running Community Maps for seven years now. They recently released the new version of Community Maps.

Get in touch with Mapping for Change, if you want to use Community Maps for your project.

EpiCollect

EpiCollect is a smart phone application to collect geographic data. Use the EpiCollect plug-in to enable data collection with EpiCollect in GeoKey.

Currently, EpiCollect is only available for Android phones.
