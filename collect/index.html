Collect data

Use these applications to connect to GeoKey and collect data with your community.

EpiCollect+

EpiCollect+ is a smart phone application to collect geographic data. It is currently available for Android phones only, but an iPhone version is in development.

Use the EpiCollect plug-in to enable data collection with EpiCollect in GeoKey.
